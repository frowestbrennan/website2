Drone Filming

Forrest has been a drone pilot since 2018, first flying the 3DR Solo. Since then he has flown bigger and more modern drones including the DJI Mavic Pro and Inspire. Most recently he has flown custom-made drones designed for carrying larger cinema cameras for VR 180 content.

While mostly flying drones for Immersive projects, Forrest also captures traditional aerial videos and photos. He's flown in environments ranging from tropical coastlines to snow-covered mountains.

Forrest is FAA Part 107 certified. He shoots establishing shots for documentaries, real estate footage, and 360 aerial captures for Immersive experiences. All aerial footage can be edited and color graded in post.
