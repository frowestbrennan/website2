Drone Filming

Forrest has been a drone pilot since 2018, first flying the 3DR Solo. Since then he has flown bigger and more modern drones including the DJI Mavic Pro and Inspire. Most recently he has flown custom-made drones designed for carrying larger cinema cameras for VR 180 content.

While mostly flying drones for Immersive projects, Forrest also captures traditional videos and photos. His aerial work spans diverse environments from tropical coastlines to snow-covered mountains, capturing unique perspectives that ground-based cameras simply cannot achieve.

As an FAA Part 107 certified drone pilot, Forrest brings technical expertise and creative vision. Whether it's an establishing shot for a documentary, real estate footage, or complex 360 aerial captures for Immersive experiences, he has the experience to capture the footage you need.
