VR For Events

Forrest creates short VR 180 and 360 videos specifically for trade shows, fundraisers, and other events. Each project includes a custom-built video player app so attendees get a seamless viewing experience on the headset — no menus, no setup, just put it on and go.

Forrest handles the entire process — filming, post-production, app development, headset setup, and on-site support. The finished experience is yours to reuse at every future event.

Forrest has produced VR event content for Bark, NASA (Girls in Space Day), and a showcase at the University of Oregon. Projects range from 90-second pieces to multi-scene interactive walkthroughs.

Your booth has 10 feet of floor space. A VR headset gives your visitors an unlimited showroom — they can step inside your facility, watch your team in action, or experience your mission firsthand.

In Action

How It Works

Plan

Forrest works with you to figure out what to film and how long the piece should be. Most event videos run 1-3 minutes.

Film

Forrest shoots VR 180 or 360 video with cinema-grade immersive cameras at your facility, event, or on location.

Edit & Build

Stitching, color grading, spatial audio, and VFX. Then Forrest builds a custom video player app so attendees just put the headset on and the video plays.

Deploy

Delivered on pre-loaded headsets ready for your booth. Forrest can be on-site to run it, or hand off a self-service kit for your team.
