App Development & Virtual Tours

Creating immersive experiences is a lot of what Forrest does, but when it comes time to show your potential customers or clients what we have made, distribution is a challenge in and of itself. Forrest creates basic video player apps for the Quest 3 for tradeshow-type experiences. He also creates larger branching narrative projects where the user can explore on their own as the self-guided story leads them to a new level of experience.

Using Unity, Forrest builds custom VR applications that run natively on headsets like the Meta Quest 3. These range from simple video players that showcase 360 and VR 180 content to fully interactive experiences with spatial UI, scene transitions, and user-driven navigation. Unity's flexibility allows for precise control over performance and user experience.

For web-based virtual tours, Forrest uses 3D Vista to create interactive experiences accessible from any browser. These tours feature hotspot navigation, embedded video and audio, floor plans, and custom branding. Perfect for real estate, hospitality, museums, and corporate presentations where users need to explore spaces remotely.

Whether you need a standalone Quest app for a trade show booth, an interactive training module, or a web-based virtual tour for your website, Forrest can deliver your immersive content on the right platform for your audience. From initial concept to final deployment, every project is tailored to meet your specific distribution goals.
