Traditional Editing

Forrest has been editing video since 2008 using Final Cut Pro and ingesting mini-DV tapes. He has edited music videos, documentaries, art films, horror films and many more. He has a passion for storytelling and enjoys short and feature films of all genres.

Forrest is also very adept at visual effects like object removal, stabilization, compositing, 3D animation and many of the VFX he applies in the Immersive space as well.

With nearly two decades of experience, Forrest brings both technical precision and creative intuition to every project. Whether you need a polished corporate video, an emotionally resonant documentary, or a visually striking narrative piece, he approaches each edit with a commitment to serving the story. His diverse background across genres means he can adapt to any style while maintaining the highest production standards.
