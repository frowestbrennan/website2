Immersive Post-Production

Processing immersive content is a multi-stage process requiring planning and experience. Forrest has overseen post-production on dozens of XR projects including editing, VFX, spatial audio, and app development.

Every immersive project starts with organization — planning each step of the post-production timeline and tracking progress to stay on schedule.

Forrest converts 360° video to dome master format for planetarium and fulldome projection — framing, tilt adjustments, and resolution optimization for curved screens. He also repurposes existing 360° footage into dome-ready content.

Beyond editing, Forrest builds interactive experiences and virtual tours for VR headsets and web using Unity and 3D Vista. Learn more about app development services →

Color grading and finishing for immersive content, making sure the final output looks right across headsets and projection systems.
