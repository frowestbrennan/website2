Live Events

Forrest has been filming live events since 2008 starting with weddings, musical/theatrical and art show openings. He can cover just about any event, capturing the meaningful moments for those who could not make it. Forrest also films immersive 360 and VR 180 live events for a truly transportive experience.

Forrest has filmed over a hundred musical shows, dozens of podcasts that want a video element for the audience and interviewed various professionals and artists alike. He also edits his footage to intercut between various camera angles for events such as SXSW and Pickathon.

His work spans live music performances, band documentaries, and cooking shows. He regularly collaborates with Prime Time Cable on podcasts, live shows, cooking programs, and documentaries.

Working under the pressure of a live event is a challenge that Forrest thrives in!
