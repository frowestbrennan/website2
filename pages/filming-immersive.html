Immersive Filming

Forrest has been filming Immersive content since 2014 when the go-to for 360 footage was GoPros attached to each other. Since then, he has filmed with every major 360 and 180 camera that has come out. Each camera has its own perks, and only from using them can a camera operator get a sense for how best to use them.

Forrest has used the Z Cam V1 Pro, Insta360 Pro 2, Insta360 Titan, Canon R5C with the Dual Fisheye lens, and the Blackmagic Ursa Immersive. From stitching and working on the post-production for all these cameras, Forrest is able to guide clients and actors to get the most pleasing results.

Immersive filming requires a different approach than traditional cinematography. Camera placement, blocking, and lighting all need to account for the 360-degree field of view. With over a decade of experience, Forrest understands how to work within these constraints while still achieving compelling shots.

From documentary to commercial Immersive projects, Forrest brings his experience from many varied projects. He has climbed steep mountains and wind turbines, swam with whale sharks, strapped a 360 camera to a pig and horse, flew a drone over the ocean of Hawaii and Thailand and captured a military training for the US Coast Guard taking down a pirate ship. He is always looking for the next adventure!
