Immersive Filming

Forrest has been filming Immersive content since 2014 when the go-to for 360 footage was GoPros attached to each other. Since then, he has filmed with every major 360 and 180 camera that has come out. Each camera has its own perks, and only from using them can a camera operator get a sense for how best to use them.

Forrest has used the Z Cam V1 Pro, Insta360 Pro 2, Insta360 Titan, Canon R5C with the Dual Fisheye lens, and the Blackmagic Ursa Immersive. From stitching and working on the post-production for all these cameras, Forrest is able to guide clients and actors to get the most pleasing results.

Immersive filming requires a different approach than traditional cinematography. Camera placement, blocking, and lighting all need to account for the 360-degree field of view. With a decade of experience, Forrest understands how to work within these constraints while still achieving compelling shots. Whether it's a narrative piece, documentary, virtual tour, or live event, Forrest brings both the technical knowledge and creative problem-solving needed to capture immersive footage that truly transports viewers.

Formats

360° Video

Full spherical capture for complete immersion. Ideal for virtual tours, documentaries, and experiences where viewers want to explore every angle.

VR 180

Stereoscopic 3D with front-facing capture. Perfect for narrative content, interviews, and experiences where depth perception enhances the story.

Dome / Fulldome

Specialized capture for planetarium and dome theater projection. Creating content that envelops audiences in immersive environments.
